Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 3, 2026

Overview

MSC2OSC is designed with privacy in mind. The app operates entirely on your local device and network, with no data collection, tracking, or external communication.

Data Collection

MSC2OSC does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No user accounts or registration required
  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No crash reporting or diagnostics sent externally
  • No cookies or persistent identifiers
  • No access to contacts, photos, or other personal files

Network Communication

MSC2OSC sends OSC (Open Sound Control) messages over UDP to an IP address and port that you configure. This communication:

  • Occurs only on your local network
  • Is outbound-only to devices you specify
  • Does not connect to the internet
  • Does not communicate with any external servers

Local Storage

The app stores your preferences (such as MIDI source selection, OSC destination, and output format settings) locally on your Mac using standard macOS preferences. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

MSC2OSC does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs. The app is fully self-contained.

Children's Privacy

MSC2OSC does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Since the app collects no data, significant changes are unlikely.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can open an issue on our GitHub repository.
